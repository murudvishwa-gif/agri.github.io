Guided walkthrough

Start with a simple field scenario and follow the decisions step by step.

The demo journey begins with a real farming situation: low moisture in one field, a weather shift in the forecast, and a harvest decision coming later in the week.

Visitors can understand how dashboard data, task planning, service support, and market preparation connect without needing technical knowledge.

Walkthrough includes

  • Field status summary with priority alerts.
  • Suggested irrigation and crop-care actions.
  • Weather timing for spraying and labour work.
  • Harvest readiness checklist for produce quality.
  • Market preparation notes before dispatch.
