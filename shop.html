Buyer guidance

Choose products based on crop stage, field size, and season.

Each product should connect to a real farming task. Soil kits support preparation, irrigation sets support dry periods, crates support harvest, and compost products support long-term fertility.

Use the shop as a practical buying guide rather than only a product list.

Before you buy

  • Check your field size and current crop stage.
  • Confirm whether the product suits soil, water, or harvest work.
  • Review quantity needs before adding to cart.
  • Plan storage for inputs and tools after delivery.
  • Contact support for product matching help.
